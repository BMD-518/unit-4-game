Crystal Collector

Instructions


At the start of each game you will be given a random number.


Below there are four crystals, by clicking each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score.


In order to win the game you must match your total score to the random number. If your score exceeds the random number you will aquire a loss.


The value of each crystal is hidden until it is clicked.


The crystal values are reset before each round.
